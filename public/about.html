About my project

AI Agent

My AI Agent is a personal virtual assistant designed to support users in daily tasks — from answering questions and remembering personal information to offering intelligent advice. Built on advanced artificial intelligence technologies, the AI Agent can “remember” information provided by the user, allowing it to personalize responses and interact in real-time.

More than just a chatbot, the AI Agent serves as a digital companion — polite, friendly, and always ready to listen. This project was developed by Đặng Hà Sang, a student at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT), with the goal of making AI more accessible and genuinely helpful to everyone.
